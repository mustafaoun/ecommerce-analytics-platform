# E-commerce Analytics Platform - Business Requirements

## Business Questions
1. What is our monthly customer retention rate?
2. Which products have the highest profit margin?
3. What are peak sales hours?
4. Which marketing channels have highest ROI?
5. What is average customer lifetime value?
6. What is inventory turnover rate?
7. What are seasonal sales patterns?

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
1. Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
3. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
4. Conversion Rate
5. Average Order Value (AOV)
6. Inventory Turnover
7. Customer Retention Rate
